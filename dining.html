A Culinary Journey

Unforgettable flavors, ocean views, and exquisite dining experiences.

Indulge in a diverse range of dining experiences at Shangri La Beach Resort. From elegant fine dining to casual beachfront bites, our restaurants and bars offer something to tantalize every taste bud and create unforgettable culinary moments.

The Beach House

Experience the epitome of culinary excellence with panoramic ocean views, locally-sourced seafood, and refined ambiance.

Cuisine: Fine Dining, Seafood, International
Ambiance: Elegant, Romantic, Oceanfront
Dress Code: Smart Casual
Reservations: Recommended

The Sand Bar

Right on the beach, enjoy grilled specialties and refreshing bites in a laid-back setting with your feet in the sand.

Cuisine: Casual, Grill, Seafood, American
Ambiance: Relaxed, Beachfront, Informal
Dress Code: Casual
Reservations: Not Required

The Poolside Cafe

Stay poolside with wraps, light snacks, and refreshing beverages to keep you fueled and refreshed all day long.

Cuisine: Light Bites, Snacks, Beverages
Ambiance: Relaxed, Poolside
Dress Code: Casual
Reservations: Not Required

The Lobby Lounge

Sophisticated cocktails, fine wines, and live music in an elegant lounge atmosphere — perfect for unwinding and socializing.

Cuisine: Beverages, Light Appetizers
Ambiance: Sophisticated, Live Music
Dress Code: Smart Casual
Reservations: Not Required

Other Dining Options

Room Service: 24/7 private in-room dining.

Buffet Breakfast: A wide variety of hot & cold dishes, fresh fruits, and pastries to start your day.

Special Dietary Needs: Let us know and we’ll cater to your needs with care.
